My research focuses on molecular dynamics simulations to study protein-ligand interactions and drug discovery mechanisms. Using computational approaches, I investigate how molecules interact at the atomic level to understand biological processes and develop potential therapeutic interventions.

I work with computational tools including GROMACS, AMBER, and various bioinformatics resources to investigate molecular-level phenomena. My goal is to contribute to the understanding of complex biological systems through computational modeling and simulation.
